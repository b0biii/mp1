Logitech G402: Hyperion Fury

Previous Next

Mouse Feet

Teflon PTFE

Sensor

Logitech Fusion Hybrid

Programmable Buttons

8 buttons

Connectivity

Rubber-coated

Description:

Made by Logitech in the year 2015. It is equipped with Logitech's own Fusion Sensor, 8 programmable buttons through the GHub Software, standard rubber-coated cable, and Teflon PTFE as its mouse feet. Its light color is only set to blue but you can cycle through different modes.

Personal Experience:

The mouse lasted 5 years for me with no issues other than the worn-out side-grip. I wouldn't have changed it if I wasn't gifted a new one. The sensor was also very reliable. I also liked its blue lighting back then so I found no issue with it being non-RGB.
