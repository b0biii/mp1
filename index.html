Welcome to Ian's Peripherals Dump!


Throughout the years, I've tried different types and kinds of computer peripherals.
I've used them mainly for the following:

Gaming

The main reason why I started to upgrade by peripherals. If you want a great gaming experience, a mouse with a reliable sensor and a responsive keyboard is a must.

Programming

Since it involves typing a lot, you need to agree with your keyboard and find the switch type, form factor, and sound that satisfies you and will help you withstand long sessions.

Productivity

I believe that having good peripherals also influence one's productivity due to the better experience it gives due to the functionalities that they come with.

ABOUT

Hi! I'm Ian Nathaniel J. Lapuz, a BS Computer Science Student in the University of the Philippines - Baguio. Given my chosen collegiate program, I code a lot so I got into upgrading my keyboard for my own typing satisfaction. I also play a lot of First Person Shooter games so I see to it that I have a mouse that have a reliable sensor. Recently, I've decided to also consider portability so that I could just use them on the fly, whenever.

Ian's Peripheral Dump serves as a blog for my current and past computer peripherals. At the moment, it only contains mice and keyboards but It'll be updated from time to time to document my upgrades. I'll also be adding other peripherals such as headphones and mics in the future.
